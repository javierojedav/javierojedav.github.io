Javier Ojeda

Ph.D. Earth and Environmental Sciences, M.Sc. Geophysics

Hola a todas y todos! This is my research site

I aim to understand earthquake processes through an integrated seismogeodetic approach, with broad interests including:

  • Characterizing (a)seismic slip behavior using geodetic and seismological observations

  • Modeling earthquake ground motions for seismic hazard and engineering applications

  • Assessing future earthquake potential from historical events

  • Investigating natural and anthropogenic processes through environmental seismology

This work has only been possible thanks to the generosity of great researchers who have guided me along my journey, Gracias!.
